About Us

Helping kids discover the story of where they live.

Welcome to Neighborhood Navigators!

We believe every neighborhood has a story worth discovering. Neighborhood Navigators creates activity boxes designed specifically for the communities children live in, turning familiar streets, landmarks, parks, and local businesses into exciting adventures.

Each box is filled with coloring pages, puzzles, games, scavenger hunts, fun facts, and hands-on activities inspired by real places in your neighborhood. Our goal is to help children develop a deeper connection to their community while encouraging creativity, curiosity, and family time.

Whether they're solving clues about a local landmark, coloring their favorite park, or learning about the history of their town, kids become explorers of the world right outside their front door.

Designed for children ages 2–10, Neighborhood Navigators transforms everyday places into opportunities for learning, imagination, and adventure.

Our Mission

To inspire children to discover, appreciate, and engage with their local communities through play-based learning and exploration.

Why We Created It

In a world where children often know more about places online than the places around them, we wanted to create something that helps families reconnect with their communities. By celebrating local landmarks, history, culture, and businesses, we're helping raise the next generation of curious, engaged community members.
